The Hidden Cost of AI

Artificial Intelligence

Many people have no idea the environmental threat that large-scale use of artificial intelligence poses. Before exploring the impact that energy consumption and water use have on the environment, it's important to understand how and why AI uses so many resources.

How AI Uses Energy and Water

AI requires data centers to function- large facilities that store and process large amounts of data. Training artificial intelligence models in AI data centers takes a lot of computing power and extremely powerful processors. These graphics processing units (GPUs) generate significant heat which requires cooling that consumes enormous amounts of water and energy.

Even the CEO of OpenAI has warnings

The CEO of OpenAI, the parent company of ChatGPT, responded to a twitter user asking how much money has been spent on electricity for ChatGPT by saying, “Tens of millions of dollars well spent — you never know”.

While this seems like a funny lesson to AI users to stop saying "please" and "thank you" so often, it reveals a deeper problem. If a handful of extra characters warrant a response from the CEO of ChatGPT's parent company, what do all the lengthy questions and queries students have for AI everyday add up to for the environment?

Proposed AI Data Center in Yspilanti

The University of Michigan has proposed the building of a new data center in Ypsilanti with Los Alamos National Laboratory. According to its website, Los Alamos addresses national security challenges while also working with the Department of Energy. The Ypsilanti Township has stated that the proposed high-performance computing facility and AI data center will focus on “science, energy, and national security” (Ypsilanti Township). Neither the University of Michigan nor Los Alamos National Laboratory has explained how an AI data center, which consumes extensive amounts of energy and water, will aid in development of energy-saving practices. Its also unclear how this facility will support the environmental management that Los Alamos National Laboratory has stated that they focus on.

“The University of Michigan and Los Alamos National Laboratory are collaborating to create a new facility for high-performance computing and AI research in Washtenaw County focused on science, energy, and national security.” - Ypsilanti Township Website

“Los Alamos National Laboratory addresses national security challenges by advancing breakthroughs in science and technology. We work alongside the Department of Energy to support energy advancements and environmental management.” - Los Alamos Website
